The house of agness / in progress

Web App



Project Overview

The project aims to create a minimal and user-friendly website that showcase the brands style, facilitate online purchases and connect international customers with their loved ones in Mozambique through the gift-giving experience.
The House of Agness is a luxury brand in Mozambique that specializes in exquisite floral designs and unique gift boxes.



Process

I am currently in the process of creating a brand-new website for The House of Agness using the Squarespace platform. Content and images will be provided by the The House of Agness. My role involves the integration of these elements into a cohesive and functional website.


I commenced by conducting interviews with The House of Agness, aiming to grasp their vision and brand essence. I then used this information to brainstorm ideas, create mood boards for visual inspiration, develop a clear information structure for the site, design wireframes to outline the layout, and seamlessly integrate their brand identity into the website.



Responsabilities

Design concept, layout and wireframing, content integration, user interface design, user experience design, squarespace customization, responsive design, e-commerce integration, project management
